Lebanese Immigration - in a Nutshell

What's going on?

Lebanon is known for its high immigration rates. This is mainly due to economic reasons, and hope for a better life elsewhere. Since 2019, Lebanon faced a stark economic collapse causing this immigration rate to increase even more than usual
